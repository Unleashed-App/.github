## Fixing Setup and Workflow Friction

Import errors: clean product SKUs, supplier names, units of measure, and stock locations before loading records into Unleashed inventory software.  

Stock mismatches: review open Unleashed sales orders, recent receipts, warehouse transfers, and adjustment history before changing counted quantities.  

Purchasing delays: confirm supplier settings, lead times, approval steps, and Unleashed purchase orders so replenishment reflects actual buying cycles.  

Integration gaps: test Unleashed integrations with accounting and ecommerce systems using a small product set before syncing the full catalog.  

Reporting confusion: align Unleashed reporting fields with finance categories, warehouse locations, and management metrics before sharing dashboards across teams.
